The 100 turn Plan

Step 0: Sam

Sam has been pretty lonely for a while. We’re trying to fix that. She’s like way way way over there, and we needed to be able to cross a few oceans to get to her. Now that we’ve seen her shores, we plan to unveil public tourist routes to go visit Sam in only a few more turns.

Step 1: Capital Refurbishment

Many citizens were confused about our capital, country, and our king, being named David. To remedy this situation we plan to rename the capital something fancy like Samuel, Richard, or Kyle.

Step 2: Theological Improvement

Many citizens, as well as foreign domestic bodies, have found our religious programs to be lacking. Starting in this year, 2020 AD (After David), we will be increasing religious funding by over 20%.

Step 3: Oceanic Exploration

As many citizens know, exploration past our own seas has been underway for several years. In the coming decades, these exploratory expeditions will be given a great deal more attention.

Step 4: The threat of Pebis

Our neighbors to the north, the kingdom of Pebis, under Tyrant Lucca has had low popularity in our region for some time. Following local protests, we will be entering diplomatic relations with tyr. Lucca in an attempt to negotiate friendly relations between their own government and peoples, as well as with our own public.
