The 100 Turn Plan

Here at Good Boys Inc. we aim to give you the best possible dog pictures. The entirety of our website, every single page, is adapted for nothing from dogs. Big dogs, small dogs, cute dogs, ugly dogs, and everything in between; we have every single type of dog that you can imagine. Look below for the specific ways to access our doggos. This page was made entirely under our own power and we are very greatful for every action you take in regards to this page. Spend as much time here as you would like.

Home Page

The home page, the page you are currently on, is the base page of Good Boys Inc. This page simply houses our welcomes and general directory descriptions. It also features our acknowledgements page and mission statement. We sincerely suggest that you visit this page first before you visit any other page on our website. In addition, we ask that any link you give to this website will direct to our home page first.

Ratings Page

Our ratings page is the main attraction of the Good Boys Inc. webpage. Here you can upload a picture of your own dog (to be implemented when we learn javascript) and see how your dog is rated on a scale of 0 to 5 stars. You will also be given a description of why your dog is given that rating. We strongly recommend that this page be the very next one you visit. If you find that you like it you may navigate to our News or About page to learn more about our very cool and awesome website.

News Page

At our news page you have the great opportunity to look at the best ever doggos ever to be found on our website. You may see the dogs rating as well as the description of the rating and picture of the wonderful doggo.
